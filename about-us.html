About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to Minibus Hire

Nationwide Minibus Hire was established by 2 experienced former minibus hire and coach hire operators. They saw a gap in the market where customers were paying over the odds for minibus hire and coach hire services. Nationwide Minibus Hire was incorporated in 2010 and has gone from strength to strength in the coach hire and minibus hire industry. We have also enabled Nationwide Minibus hire to become the number 1 hire for passenger transportation across all UK towns and cities.

We have enabledour customers to save an average of 35% by booking their passenger transportation through Nationwide Minibus Hire. We have saved customers millions of pounds on their transportation. We have carried out in excess of 10,000 journeys for customer across the business and private sectors.

Why Choose Us

Professional Service

Our dedicated minibus hire and coach hire transport managers will make sure you get the most professional service all year round.

Coach Hire Services

With over 100 coaches at our disposal we have are one of London’s largest coach hire provider and we have over 50 minibuses

We monitor prices from competition on a monthly basis to ensure our minibus and coach hire prices are very competitive.
